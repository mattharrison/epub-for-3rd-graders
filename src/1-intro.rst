History
--------

* 9th Century - Chinese print books
* 1440 - Gutenberg press
* 1663 - Magazine in Germany
* 1690 - American Newspaper
* 1845 - Paperbacks
* 1971 - First ebook (Declaration)
* 2007 - Amazon Kindle
